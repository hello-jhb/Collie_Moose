# Debt Guidance

Debt facts may live on summary, assumptions, sources and uses, debt, or cash flow sheets. Debt service, interest expense, principal paydown, amortization, and monthly payments are not the same as total debt proceeds or loan amount.

When selecting debt facts, distinguish:
- loan amount / debt proceeds / senior debt
- LTV or LTC
- interest rate
- amortization
- debt service
- payoff / paydown / repayment
- refinancing proceeds

For each debt claim, cite the selected cell and explain whether it is a capital structure amount, a periodic payment, or a financing term.

If units are displayed in thousands or millions, preserve the interpreted value and note the scale context.
